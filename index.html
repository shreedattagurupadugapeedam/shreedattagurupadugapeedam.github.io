The Peedam
Our Sacred Heritage

Sri Datta Guru Paduka Peedam is a sacred spiritual centre established in the living tradition of Lord Dattatreya — the divine trinity of Brahma, Vishnu, and Shiva united in one form. The Peedam exists as a place where seekers of all paths may draw near to the eternal flame of Guru's grace.

The Paduka — the holy sandals of the Guru — are venerated here as the very presence of the living Master. Through the worship of the Padukas, devotees receive divine blessings, protection on their spiritual journey, and the dissolution of obstacles placed by karma.

Our centre welcomes all souls regardless of background or tradition. Whether you come for the first time or have walked this path for decades, the Guru's grace makes no distinction. The door of the Peedam is always open.

🔱

Lord Dattatreya — The Adi Guru

Dattatreya is revered as the first Guru of all creation — born to sage Atri and Anasuya, embodying the combined essence of the Trimurti. He is depicted with three heads representing Brahma, Vishnu, and Shiva, accompanied by four dogs and a cow, symbolising the four Vedas and the earth. His teaching is the teaching of life itself.
